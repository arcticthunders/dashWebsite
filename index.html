About Accelerate Dash

A multi-platform digital tool enabling educational institutes of all scales to attain their quality goals. A simple , user-friendly tool that facilitates optimal usage of thier resources by ensuring parent-teacher involvement in developing a nurturing and productive environment for the student. Accelerate Dash is a universal tool developed considering all aspects of instituitions of all kinds.
